About Platform

Community and Marketplace for alternative subcultures

Althemy is a community and marketplace for alternative subcultures. It is an online platform that provides the alternative community all the necessary tools they need to build their online identity and gain recognition - a website, e-commerce solution, gallery and many other features that will attract highly targeted visitors. What is most important, all these tools are integrated and serve the purpose of engaging the users and encouraging communication, discussions and trade within the community..

Networking and Tools

Powerful Social Networking and Tools for Artists & Alternative Communities

Whether you are a public figure, crafter, small business or a fan of some particular alternative genre, Althemy brings you the power of social networking and marketplace to help you reach your goals. Designed by Alternatives for Alternatives, Althemy gives you the specific set of features that will enable you to grow your site, your e-shop, to grow your base of followers, to share your goals or promote your business.
